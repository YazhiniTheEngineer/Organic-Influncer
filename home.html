Our Products

Our product category offers a wide range of natural and organic fertilizers and plant protectors that are suitable for all types of plants. Each product contains a unique blend of natural ingredients that helps promote healthy plant growth and development while also enhancing soil fertility and structure.

Bloomup

Our Bloomup is a natural and organic fertilizer derived from seaweed, which is rich in nutrients and minerals. It stimulates plant growth, enhances root development, and improves stress tolerance in plants. Bloomup can be applied to a variety of plants and helps improve soil health and structure while increasing resistance to pests and diseases. It is easy to use and can be applied via drenching or foliar spray.

Degradaste

Degradaste is an organic waste decomposer that breaks down organic waste into nutrient-rich compost. It contains beneficial microorganisms, such as bacteria and fungi, that help break down organic matter using enzymes and release essential nutrients for plant growth. This organic fertilizer improves soil structure, fertility, and water retention, and provides a rich source of nutrients for plant growth. It can be used to decompose waste from the kitchen, garden, and cattle dung, and the resulting compost can be added directly to the roots of plants or mixed with soil or coco peat. Degradaste should be stored in a cool place away from direct sunlight and heat for optimal effectiveness.

Phytofight

Our Phytofight is an organic plant disease preventer that helps prevent plant diseases without the use of harmful chemicals. It contains natural compounds that boost the plant's immune system, making it more resistant to diseases and pests. Phytofight should be applied at the first sign of disease symptoms via foliar spray or drenching. It helps protect the environment by reducing the use of harmful chemicals, making it suitable for sustainable organic farming.

Nitrogen Biofertilizer

Our nitrogen biofertilizer contains nitrogen-fixing bacteria that convert atmospheric nitrogen into a form that can be readily absorbed by plants. It provides essential nitrogen nutrients required for growth and development, which results in increased plant growth, crop yield, and quality. The product can be applied near the root zone of standing crops or used in foliar applications. Nitrogen can be mixed with phosphorous and potassium of Organic Influencer for better yield. It is compatible with most biological formulations and should be stored in a cool place away from direct sunlight and heat.

Phosphorous Biofertilizer

Our phosphorous biofertilizer contains living microorganisms that solubilize and mobilize phosphorus, making it more available to plants. It enhances root development, increases plant growth, and yields while reducing the need for synthetic fertilizers. It is compatible with most biological formulations and should not be mixed with other agro-chemical products. Phosphorous can be mixed with nitrogen and potassium of Organic Influencer for better yield.

Potassium Biofertilizer

Our potassium biofertilizer contains beneficial microorganisms that solubilize and mobilize potassium, making it more available to plants. It helps improve plant growth, root development, and stress tolerance, resulting in higher crop yields. It is compatible with most biological formulations and should not be mixed with other agro-chemical products. Potassium can be mixed with nitrogen and phosphorous of Organic Influencer for better yield.
